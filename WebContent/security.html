Security

tensbit.com delivers products only on cash on delivery option. We don't support any online payment as of now and also prefer the first option in near future. With cash on delivery user has no worries of online payment. This step has been taken to reduce the amount of online banking scams throughout our customer community.
Even if the payment option is safe but you should always follow these rules to keep your shopping account safe from other's interference.

  • Never Share your password with anyone.
  • Link you account to only your mail account not any social media websites.
  • Choose a password that would be hard to break.
  • Always Logout after you are done.

If you follow those things then your account will be safe. But still if you are experiencing security issues, feel free to contact us.
